GUIDES

  • FAMILIARIZE YOURSELF WITH YOUR DEVICE
  • Before you start vaping, make sure you know how your device works. Read the user manual, watch tutorials online, or ask a knowledgeable friend to walk you through it.
  • CHARGE YOUR BATTERY
  • If your device has a rechargeable battery, make sure it's fully charged before you start using it. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for charging your device.
  • FILL YOUR TANK
  • If you're using a device with a tank, fill it up with your chosen e-liquid. Make sure to avoid overfilling the tank, as this can cause leaks.
  • PRIME YOUR COIL
  • If you're using a device with replaceable coils, it's important to prime the coil before you start vaping. This involves saturating the wick with e-liquid to prevent dry hits and burnt tastes. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for priming your coil.
  • TURN ON YOUR DEVICE
  • Most vaping devices have a button or switch to turn them on. Make sure your device is turned on before you start vaping.
  • TAKE A PUFF
  • Place your lips around the mouthpiece and take a slow, steady puff. Depending on the type of device you're using, you may need to press a button or inhale to activate the heating element.
  • EXPERIMENT WITH VAPING TECHNIQUES
  • Different devices and e-liquids require different vaping techniques. Try experimenting with direct-to-lung (DTL) or mouth-to-lung (MTL) inhales, as well as different power settings and airflow options.
  • MONITOR YOUR BATTERY LIFE
  • Keep an eye on your device's battery life and recharge it as needed. Overusing your device or letting the battery run too low can damage your device.
  • CLEAN YOUR DEVICE REGULARLY
  • Regular cleaning can help prolong the life of your device and prevent build-up of e-liquid or other debris. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for cleaning your device.
  • STAY SAFE
  • Follow all safety guidelines when using your vaping device, including avoiding overuse, keeping your device away from children and pets, and avoiding vaping in restricted areas.
